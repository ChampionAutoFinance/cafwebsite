How It Works

From application to funded

Two clear paths, one Champion standard — one for dealers submitting deals, one for buyers financing a lease buyout. Pick yours below.

For Dealers → Lease Buyouts
For Dealers

How dealer deals work

Submit once and we take it from there — matching, stipulations, compliance, and funding.

1

Structure the deal

Enter the customer, vehicle, and terms in a dealer-first workspace — one organized worksheet, no scattered faxes or paperwork.

2

We shop your lenders

Your deal is matched across our network of qualified lenders and credit tiers to find the strongest path to approval.

3

Stips handled

We coordinate stipulations, documentation, and compliance so the file is airtight and fully compliant before it funds.

4

Funded & paid

The approval funds, the customer drives, and you get paid — quick and easy.

For Lease Buyouts

How lease buyouts work

Keep the car you already know and lock in terms that make sense — no obligation to start.

1

Tell us about the car

Share your lease payoff details and a little about your credit. It takes minutes and costs nothing.

2

We match it to lenders

We review the numbers and shop your file across qualified lenders and credit tiers to find the right structure.

3

Review clear terms

You see the rate, payment, and term explained in plain language — with no hidden fees or pressure.

4

Sign & keep driving

Documents are handled smoothly and compliantly. The payoff is settled and the car is yours on new terms.

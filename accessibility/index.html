Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

We want every visitor — including people who use assistive technology — to be able to use championautofinance.com.

Effective date: July 17, 2026

Our commitment

Champion Auto Finance is committed to making our website accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of ability or technology. We believe everyone should be able to learn about dealer financing and lease buyouts and reach us easily.

Standard we aim for

We work to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines are the widely recognized benchmark for making web content more accessible to people with a broad range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive impairments.

What we have done

  • Text and background colors are chosen to meet AA contrast ratios.
  • Images that convey meaning include descriptive alternative text.
  • Forms use programmatic labels so they work with screen readers.
  • The site is operable with a keyboard, with a visible focus indicator and a “skip to content” link.
  • Animation is reduced automatically for visitors who set a “reduce motion” preference.
  • Pages use clear headings, descriptive titles, and semantic structure.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing process, not a one-time task. We test our site periodically, and we continue to improve it as standards, browsers, and assistive technologies evolve. Some content may not yet be fully accessible, and we are actively working to address any gaps.

Report a barrier — we want to hear from you

If you encounter any difficulty using this website, or need information in a different format, please tell us. We will do our best to provide the information or complete the transaction with you through another channel, and to fix the issue.
